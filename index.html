M.S. Dhoni

The Captain Cool
 
 
 

    Here's a timeline of M.S. Dhoni's career:

  • 1981 - Mahendra Singh Dhoni was born on 7th July, 1981.
    •        
  • 2004 - The international journey of the biggest finisher in the world cricket began on 23rd December, 2004.
  • 2006 - On 1st December, 2006, Dhoni played his first international T20.
  • 2007 - India lift the first ever T20 World Cup under the leadership of Dhoni.
  • 2009 - In 2009, India soar up to the number 1 ranking in test cricket for the first time in their history.
  • 2011 - India wins the World Cup held in India in 2011 after 28 years of their first world cup triumph and Dhoni led the way with an exquisite 91* in the final game against Sri Lanka.
  • 2013 - India win their third ICC title under the captaincy of MS Dhoni in form of Champions Trophy.
  • 2014 - Dhoni retires from Test Cricket in middle of India's tour of Australia after sixth consecetive away series loss as captain.
  • 2015 - India reach the semi-finals of 2015 cricket World Cup held in Australia & New Zealand.
  • 2017 - Dhoni steps down as captain of Indian Cricket Team from all forms of cricket.
  • 2017 - Current - Dhoni continues to play limited-overs cricket for India and offerinf his services to Indian Cricket.

"M S Dhoni is the best captain I have played under."
